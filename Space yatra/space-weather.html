What is Space Weather?

Discover the amazing world of space weather and how it affects our planet!

Solar Flares

NASA Solar Flare Image

Solar flares are sudden bursts of energy from the Sun! They're like giant explosions that release light, X-rays, and particles into space.

Fun Fact: A single solar flare can release as much energy as millions of atomic bombs!

Coronal Mass Ejections (CMEs)

NASA CME Image

CMEs are huge clouds of solar particles that the Sun shoots into space! They can travel millions of miles and reach Earth in just a few days.

Fun Fact: CMEs can be bigger than the Sun itself!

Solar Wind

NASA Solar Wind Visualization

The solar wind is a stream of charged particles constantly flowing from the Sun! It's like a gentle breeze, but made of tiny particles.

Fun Fact: The solar wind travels at speeds of up to 500 miles per second!

What Happens When Space Weather Reaches Earth?

Satellites

Space weather can affect satellites and make them work differently!

Radio Signals

It can make radio signals weaker or stronger!

Aurora

It creates beautiful lights in the sky called aurora!

Power Grids

It can sometimes affect power lines on Earth!
